Where are the abortion clinics located?

Abortion clinics vs. anti-abortion clinics

In Mexico City there are 13 public clinics and hospitals that provide abortions. Due to the pandemic, only nine of them are providing this service. This map shows the location of the nine clinics that are currently providing abortion services and the eight anti-abortion clinics located mostly infront of the public clinics. Click on the icon markers to get more information. On the top right corner you can filter out by type of clinic.

Accesibility to abortion clinics

This map shows the public and private clinics by accesibility and capacity. The 46 private clinics are ranked by distance to a metro station. The public clinics are ranked by the number of abortions provided since 2007, assuming that the bigger the number, the higher the capacity. Click on each circle to see more information. On the top right corner you can filter out by type of clinic.
